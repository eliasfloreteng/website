This document, "The 4-Hour Body," explores **optimisation across various aspects of human performance and health**. It introduces the **Pareto Principle (80/20 rule)** as a guiding philosophy, focusing on the "minimum effective dose" for maximum results in areas such as **fat loss and muscle gain**. The text covers practical applications like the **Slow-Carb Diet**, **targeted exercise routines (e.g., kettlebell swings, high-intensity training, sprinting mechanics)**, and **strategies for injury prevention and recovery**. Furthermore, it touches upon **sexual performance, sleep optimisation, and longevity**, all while advocating for **data-driven self-experimentation** and critical evaluation of scientific claims.
